Nine years after Elon Musk promised a Tesla would drive itself coast-to-coast, we finally made it happen.

## The Achievement

Alongside **Alex Roy** and **Paul Pham**, I just completed the first-ever zero-intervention Tesla Full Self-Driving trip from Los Angeles to New York City.

**The numbers:**
- **3,081 miles** in **58 hours, 22 minutes**

## The Journey

The route followed I-10 from Redondo Beach through Arizona, then I-40 toward Oklahoma City and St. Louis before cutting northeast into a Midwest winter storm.
