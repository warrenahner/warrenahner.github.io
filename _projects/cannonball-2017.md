## The Record

In 2017, we broke both the electric vehicle and autonomous Cannonball Run records in a Tesla, demonstrating the potential of electric and semi-autonomous vehicles for long-distance travel.

This was the predecessor run that laid the groundwork for our eventual zero-intervention achievement in 2026.
