## The Numbers

| | |
|---|---|
| **Distance** | 3,081 miles |
| **Time** | 58 hours, 22 minutes |
| **Interventions** | Zero |
| **Hardware** | Tesla Model S, HW4, FSD v14.2.2.3 |
| **Charging time** | 10 hours, 11 minutes |

## The Rules

Simple: nobody touches the wheel. Not to correct a lane change. Not to speed up. Not even during charging stops.

## The Weather

We drove into a winter storm. Not around it. *Through* it.

The route took us on I-10 through Arizona, then I-40 toward Oklahoma City and St. Louis. By the time we hit the Midwest, weather radar was showing red everywhere. Snow squalls. Icy roads. Visibility that dropped to nothing.

FSD kept driving.

## The Rest Stop Incident

Somewhere in western Pennsylvania, we stopped for a bathroom break. One team member got out... and the car drove away.

Rather than disengage FSD to turn around, we completed a **90-minute detour** to circle back and pick him up.

That's commitment.
